Total volatility

Our news ecosystem is mostly negative and this increases its volatility.

Negative 46%
Positive 36%
Neutral 19%

See how we calculate these scores and check our codebook for more information.

News volatility follows news article volume. As news is produced at higher levels, emotional volatility becomes heightened and the gap between Negative and Positive stories increases.

This type of behavior is present in systems that are easily manipulated, even when there are tight controls and regulations in place, including the stock market. In fact, this phenomenon partially mirrors something in market manipulation called the "pump-and-dump" scheme.
